Carbonara

Carbonara recipe

Ingredient list

  • 2 Eggs
  • 400g guanchale
  • 150g parmesan
  • 200g spaghetti

Carbonara is an italian spaghetti recipe consisting of guanchale (panchetta or bacon as substitutes),

and a sauce made from the pasta water starch, eggs and parmesan cheese

  1. Boil water for the spaghetti, remember to season the water with salt
  2. While the water is boiling, cube your guanchale and fry it off in a pan
  3. Add spaghetti to water when boiling
  4. Whisk the eggs and cheese together in a bowl
  5. Take the guanchale off the heat, and add spaghetti when its cooked
  6. Add the cheese/egg mixture and whisk rigorously
  7. Enjoy with extra cheese, fresh pepper and salt to taste
